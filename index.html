About me

The science of engagement.

My journey didn't start in a marketing boardroom; it started behind a lens and in the laboratory. As an Evolutionary Biologist in training, I quickly realized that the most groundbreaking programs fail if they cannot communicate their value to the people who matter most: the students.

Over the last 9 years, I have honed my skills as a visual storyteller and content strategist. Now, navigating my own path as an international MSc student at the University of Helsinki, I intimately understand the challenges, questions, and opportunities new students face when arriving at the Faculty of Agriculture and Forestry.

Whether it is planning impactful website news, managing active social media channels, or organizing group-building activities for multidisciplinary cohorts, my goal is to leverage my content creation expertise to make the student journey seamless, inclusive, and inspiring.
